Previous findings

  • The inclusion of real cohort IDs into the training improves the prediction accuracy.

  • Response-clustering algorithm is able to predict the cohort IDs with improved accuracy.
  • Prediction accuracy for seen users was observed to be improved.

Learning rate story

Findings

  • It is safe to say that using reduced-user response as features gives comparable performance to response-clustering approach.
  • However, the response-clustering algorithm bags on its interpretability.
  • The algorithm is not auto regressive as each campaign vector is randomly and independently drawn.
  • With increasing campaigns the reduced-user response method might become computationally demanding
