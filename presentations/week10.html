Training vs. Testing

  • Decrease in accuracy doesn't seem to be due to over-fitting.

Summary of our work

  • We have developed pipeline to generate data with cohort structure.
  • We have found that when there is cohort structure in the data, training xgb model with cohort ID can improve accuracy by providing information about the hidden features. This improvement is dependent on the relative variance of visible and hidden features.
  • We have found by using previous response as features, one can infer the cohort ID and obtain improved accuracy.
  • We have found that the improvement from inferred cohort ID cannot be replicated by simply training the model on previous responses.
